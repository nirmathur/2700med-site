Our Approach
We don't just tutor.
We plan the whole thing.

Most tutoring is reactive. We build a complete system around each student, combining smart planning with expert human instruction at every stage of the journey.

01

Assess

Every student begins with a diagnostic assessment. We look at where you are, where you want to go, and what it'll take to get there. This is how we build a plan that actually works for you.

02

Map

We build a week-by-week roadmap spanning the entire admissions timeline: subject targets, UCAT milestones, personal statement drafts, work experience windows, and interview preparation blocks.

03

Execute

Each student is matched with tutors selected specifically for their needs. Weekly sessions drill concepts, run mock MMIs, and review practice exams. We track performance and adapt the plan as you progress.

04

Deliver

By the time UCAS results arrive, every element of the application has been rehearsed, refined, and pressure-tested. Our students walk into interviews prepared, polished, and confident.

Services
Every stage of the pipeline

Whether you're just starting to think about medicine or dentistry, or deep into your application year, every service is delivered by specialist tutors and coordinated as part of your overall plan.

S

Subject Tutoring

A-level, IB, and IGCSE Biology, Chemistry, Maths, and more. Not generic tutoring. Targeted at the specific grades and knowledge your medicine and dentistry applications require.

U

UCAT Preparation

Strategy-first coaching from tutors who've been through it themselves. Timed drills, section-specific techniques, and full mock exams under real test conditions.

P

Personal Statement

Iterative drafting across multiple rounds with structural feedback. We help you articulate genuine motivation, not a templated answer that reads like everyone else's.

W

Work Experience

Guidance on securing clinical and non-clinical placements. Structured reflection frameworks that transform experiences into compelling evidence for interviews and statements.

I

Interview & MMI

Full mock MMI circuits, panel interview practice, ethics scenarios, communication coaching, and role play exercises with trained medical students.

C

UCAS Strategy

Data-informed school selection based on your UCAT score, predicted grades, and individual profile. We help you make the most of your UCAS choices for medicine and dentistry, and any international applications.

Your Tutors
Handpicked for you,
not randomly assigned

Every tutor in the 2700 network is a medical student or doctor who has personally been through the admissions process. But we go further: each student is matched with tutors selected specifically for their needs and target country.

After your screening assessment, we identify exact skill gaps and assign tutors with proven strength in those areas, whether that's UCAT Verbal Reasoning or A-level organic chemistry.

Personality and learning style

Some students need encouragement, others need directness. We consider communication style and personality in every match to ensure the relationship works.

Continuity across the journey

Your core tutor stays with you from enrolment through offer day. They know your case, your progress, and your story, not just the topic of today's session.
